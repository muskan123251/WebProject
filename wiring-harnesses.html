Engine Wiring Harness

Ensures smooth connection between engine sensors and control units for optimal performance. Designed to handle heat, vibration, and high-load conditions reliably.

Keeps network and power cables neatly arranged inside server racks. Improves airflow, reduces heat buildup, and enhances system performance. Perfect for data centers, IT rooms, and networking setups.
